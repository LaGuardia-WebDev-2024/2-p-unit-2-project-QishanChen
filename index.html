Hawaii

The Island of Hawaiʻi is best known for being the only island where you can see lava (Hawaiʻi Volcanoes National Park), its unique black and green sand beaches, wide-open scenic drives, the tallest point in Hawaiʻi (Mauna Kea), the stunning Kohala Mountains, and the renowned Kona Coast.
